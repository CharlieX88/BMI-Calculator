PRECAUTIONS

FOR UNDERWEIGHT

If your BMI is 17 or below (Underweight)

Risks:

  • Weak immune system
  • Bone weakness & high fracture risk
  • Poor growth (especially in teenagers)

Solutions:

  • Use healthy fats (olive oil, peanut butter, avocado)
  • Add protein to shakes & meals
  • Eat calorie-rich food smartly
  • Do strength training to build muscle

FOR OVERWEIGHT

If your BMI is 25 or above (Overweight/Obesity)

Risks:

  • High blood pressure
  • Joint and back pain
  • Low stamina & fatigue
  • Stress, anxiety or depression

Solutions:

  • Eat balanced foods (vegetables, protein, whole grains)
  • Drink only water, avoid sugary drinks
  • Walk or workout at least 30–45 mins daily
  • Focus on lifestyle, not just “weight loss”
